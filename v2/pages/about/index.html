About

CodeTheCurrent

A classroom platform for building courses, guiding student lessons, and reviewing learning work in one place.

What this platform is for

CodeTheCurrent helps students move through structured courses while teachers build lessons, manage classrooms, and review submitted work.

For students

Students can join courses, continue lessons, save drafts, submit work, and track learning progress.

For teachers

Teachers can create course pages, add lesson content, organize classrooms, and give feedback on student work.
