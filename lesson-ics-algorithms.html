Lesson 01

Algorithms and Problem Solving

Objective: Define an algorithm and explain how step-by-step logic supports problem solving.

Quick summary

In this lesson, students explore what an algorithm is, how it organizes thinking, and why clear sequences matter in both computers and everyday tasks.

Questions

  1. What is an algorithm in your own words?
  2. Why does the order of steps matter in an algorithm?
  3. Describe one real-world task that can be explained as an algorithm.
