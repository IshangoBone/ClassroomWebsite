Course overview

Intro to Engineering Design

This course is organized into 14 modules and 180 total lessons. The structure follows the engineering textbook chapter flow while expanding each chapter into a full year-long lesson pathway.

What students will learn

  • How engineering has developed across history and how design tools support brainstorming, sketching, modeling, and communication.
  • How mechanical advantage, mechanisms, energy, electrical systems, fluid power, and control systems shape real engineering solutions.
  • How to analyze materials, manufacturing, statics, motion, measurement, statistics, and quality in a full year-long engineering pathway.

Course snapshot

Audience Students beginning engineering design, modeling, and project-based problem solving.
Structure 14 modules aligned to the engineering textbook chapters and expanded into a year-long sequence.
Lesson count 180 lessons total
Lesson format Objective, video, summary, and guided before/during/reflection questions.
