Course overview

Intro to Engineering Design

This course is organized into 30 modules and 180 total lessons. Students move through a full engineering pathway that builds design thinking, sketching, prototyping, testing, systems understanding, and communication.

What students will learn

  • How to define problems, research needs, and move through a structured design process.
  • How to generate ideas, build prototypes, test solutions, and iterate with evidence.
  • How to communicate engineering thinking through sketches, models, documentation, and presentations.

Course snapshot

Audience Students beginning engineering design, modeling, and project-based problem solving.
Structure 30 modules organized around the engineering workflow and core technical skills.
Lesson count 180 lessons total
Lesson format Objective, video, summary, and guided before/during/reflection questions.
