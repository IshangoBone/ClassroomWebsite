Course overview

Intro to Engineering Design

Learn how engineers solve problems through research, ideation, prototyping, testing, and revision. This course is built to help students think like designers and makers.

What students will learn

  • How to move through the engineering design process with intention.
  • How constraints, criteria, and iteration shape a strong solution.
  • How prototypes and testing improve design decisions.

Course snapshot

Audience Students beginning engineering design and project-based problem solving.
