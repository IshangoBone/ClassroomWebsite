About the platform

CodeTheCurrent is built to make technical learning clearer and more accessible.

This site is designed as a modern course-delivery hub where students can move from a course overview into structured, prerecorded lessons with minimal confusion.

What the platform is for

CodeTheCurrent is meant to deliver practical, visual, and structured technical education through clean course pages and lesson pages. The early focus is simple: help students access lessons, learn clearly, and move through a repeatable course path.

Teaching focus

The site currently centers on three core technical pathways: introductory computer science, engineering design, and AP Computer Science A. Together, those courses build a strong technical identity.

Why this structure matters

  • Students can quickly understand what a course covers.
  • Each lesson follows the same predictable, low-friction format.
  • Students can return to this site anytime to review lessons and course materials.

CodeTheCurrent is a course platform for modern technical education.
