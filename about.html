About the Teacher

Hello! I’m passionate about helping students grow as thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers.

Through courses in engineering, computer science, and personal finance, I want students to build skills they can use both in and beyond the classroom.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe students learn best when they are challenged to think critically, collaborate with others, and apply their knowledge in meaningful ways.

My goal is to create a classroom environment that is engaging, supportive, and focused on curiosity, confidence, and growth.

What Students Can Expect

  • Hands-on learning and problem-solving
  • Opportunities to build technical and real-world skills
  • A classroom culture built on respect, effort, and growth
