Course overview

Intro to Computer Science

Build a foundation in computer science through clear lessons on algorithms, logic, problem solving, and beginner-friendly programming ideas.

What students will learn

  • How algorithms and logical processes drive computing systems.
  • How to break larger problems into simpler steps and patterns.
  • How foundational programming ideas connect to real-world technology.
