Lesson 01

The Engineering Design Process

Objective: Understand the major steps engineers use to move from a problem to a tested solution.

Quick summary

This lesson introduces the full engineering design cycle: identify the problem, research, imagine ideas, plan a solution, build a prototype, test it, and improve it.

Questions

  1. Why is it important to define the problem clearly before building a solution?
  2. What is the difference between criteria and constraints?
  3. Why is testing a necessary part of engineering design?
